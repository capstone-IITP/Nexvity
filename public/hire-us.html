Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with us

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites typically take 4-6 weeks, while more complex platforms may take 2-3 months. We'll provide a specific timeline in your proposal.

What is your payment structure?

+

We typically work with a 50% deposit to begin, with the remaining balance due upon completion. For larger projects, we may structure payments around key milestones.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

+

Yes, we offer maintenance packages to keep your site secure, updated, and running smoothly. We can customize a plan based on your specific needs.

How do you handle revisions?

+

Our process includes designated revision rounds at key stages. Additional revisions beyond the scope can be accommodated at an hourly rate.
